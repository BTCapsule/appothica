How to Install
PWA on Android




Chrome and
Most Browsers:

Tap the Settings icon more_vert in
the top-right corner and
choose Add to Home Screen



Samsung Internet:

Tap the Menu icon menu in the
bottom-right, click on
Add Page To, and
choose Home Screen





*Some PWA features may be
unavailable with Forefox browser
