Why Appothica?


Appothica was created to deliver the best Progressive Web Apps (PWA), and make them easily accessible for everyone. When you choose an app on Appothica, you can be sure that it will look and
feel like a native app.

We do not accept any website onto our platform. There are many great PWAs that lack certain features- such as full-screen capability- and Appothica is working hard to contact these site owners about implementing these components.










*Appothica is not required to install Progressive Web Apps
